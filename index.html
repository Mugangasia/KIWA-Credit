Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I borrow from Kiwa Credit?

As a first-time borrower, you can access loans from KSh 1,000 up to KSh 10,000. Over time, as you build a positive repayment history with us, your loan limit can increase up to KSh 50,000.

How long does it take to process a loan?

Our loan processing is automated and typically takes just minutes after application. Most approved loans are disbursed to your M-Pesa account within 5-10 minutes, depending on network conditions.

What are the interest rates and fees?

Our interest rates range from 7.5% to 15% per month, depending on your loan amount and credit score. We have a one-time processing fee of 5% of the loan amount. All fees and charges are transparently communicated before you confirm your loan.

What happens if I cannot repay my loan on time?

We understand that financial difficulties can arise. If you anticipate problems with repayment, please contact our customer service team at least 48 hours before your due date. Late repayments may attract additional fees and affect your future borrowing capacity.

How do I increase my loan limit?

Your loan limit increases automatically based on your repayment history. Timely repayments of multiple loans will gradually increase your borrowing capacity. You can also provide proof of regular income to qualify for higher loan amounts.
